# SQL + Power BI E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project combines SQL and Power BI to analyze e-commerce sales performance using the Brazilian Olist marketplace dataset.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through SQL-based analysis and interactive Power BI visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large amounts of transactional data. Without proper analysis, identifying sales trends, customer behavior, revenue drivers, and business opportunities becomes difficult.

This project provides an interactive Business Intelligence solution that helps decision-makers monitor key metrics and uncover valuable insights.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance
* Monitor revenue trends
* Evaluate customer activity
* Identify top-performing product categories
* Understand payment behavior
* Compare regional sales performance
* Support data-driven decision making

---

## Key Insights

* Credit Card payments accounted for the majority of transactions.
* Health & Beauty emerged as one of the top-performing categories.
* São Paulo generated the highest sales revenue.
* Revenue showed strong growth across the analyzed period.
* A small number of categories contributed a significant portion of total revenue.
